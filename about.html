Welcome to MedPro Health Providers

MedPro Health Providers also provides nursing, therapy, aide and specialty care to the Medicare-covered population throughout Illinois. Medicare home care services are extended to those under a physician’s care when the physician orders skilled, intermittent services and when the Medicare recipient is homebound due to illness or injury and cannot leave their residence without considerable taxing effort.

Our services also include assistance in the arrangement of supplies and equipment required to meet your individual needs. Our experienced registered nurses will design a care plan that takes into consideration your physical and financial situation. And many of our services are covered by employer group health plans, various government-funded programs, private insurance plans, Medicare or workers compensation.
